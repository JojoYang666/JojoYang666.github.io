857. Minimum Cost to Hire K Workers

2020-11-24 Leetcode PV:

Every worker in the paid group should be paid in the ratio of their quality compared to other workers in the paid group

  1. quality[i]:qulity[j] = wage[i]:wage[j](the wage here is the wage payed in the group, not in the input) ===> wage[i]:quality[i] = wage[j]:quality[j]

  2. 所以ratio(wage/quality)是一个定值， 我们要找到最小的

Every worker in the paid group must be paid at least their minimum wage expectation.”
For every worker, he has an expected ratio of wage compared to his quality.

  1. 要满足所有人的wage expectation
  2. 所以需要求的那个最低的薪水能力比例值不能小于任何一个员工自己的比例值
    • 当员工能力值越低，期望薪水越高的时候，其薪水能力比例值就越大，所以可以根据薪水能力比例值从大到小来排列员工

749. Contain Virus

2020-11-10 Leetcode PV:

while True
regions: connected virus region
fronters: all the non infected place region[i] can reach to
perimeters: the perimeters: the primiter of region[i]

dfs –> update regions, calculate fronters & perimeters of current region
build wall on the largest fronters
update grid for infecting position on current day

—> break loop until there is no new regions added

also remember to update seen to empty for each iteration!!!
